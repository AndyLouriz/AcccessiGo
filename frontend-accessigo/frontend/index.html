    About AccessiGo

    Empowering persons with disabilities through community-driven accessibility mapping in Barangay Sta. Rita, Olongapo City.

    Crowdsourced Mapping

    Every resident can contribute. Report ramps, elevators, hazards, and accessible paths. Verified by the community, kept current by the community.

    Audio Guidance

    Real-time spoken navigation for persons with visual impairments — announcing nearby features, hazards, and turn-by-turn directions in Filipino and English.

    Accessible Routes

    Routes prioritize wheelchair-friendly paths, avoiding stairs, broken surfaces, and obstacles — rated and verified by community members.

    Community Driven

    Built with and for PWDs, senior citizens, and caregivers of Barangay Sta. Rita. Every pin is a community member making a difference.
